Our Process

01

Discovery

Your market, your consumer, your thesis. No formula gets written until we understand why this product deserves to exist.

02

Formulation

Bench trials, stability testing, iteration. A formula isn't done when it works — it's done when it's the best version of itself.

03

Production

Bench to batch, scaled with precision. Every run documented, every output consistent, every unit identical to the last.

04

Launch

Market-ready, shelf-stable, fully compliant — packaged, labeled, and delivered on the date we agreed to.
