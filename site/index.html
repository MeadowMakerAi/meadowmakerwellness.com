Our Process

01

Discovery

We learn your market, your consumer, your thesis. No formula gets written until we understand why this product needs to exist.

02

Formulation

Bench trials, stability testing, iteration. The formula isn't done when it works — it's done when it's the best version of itself.

03

Production

Bench to batch, scaled with precision. Every run documented, every output consistent, every unit identical to the one before it.

04

Launch

Market-ready, shelf-stable, fully compliant. Packaged, labeled, and delivered on the date we agreed to.

We are a product studio, not a contract manufacturer.

The difference matters. A contract manufacturer fills your order. We interrogate your idea, pressure-test your formula, redesign what isn't working, and deliver a product that's genuinely better than what you walked in with.

Adaptogens, nootropics, functional botanicals, novel extracts — we work across the full spectrum of interesting compounds. The throughline is design intelligence: every decision, from active ingredient sourcing to packaging substrate, is made with intention.
